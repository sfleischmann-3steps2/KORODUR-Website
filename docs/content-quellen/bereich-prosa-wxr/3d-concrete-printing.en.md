# 3D Concrete Printing

## save time. save cost. be sustainable.

KORODUR is a manufacturer of cementitious products that has been producing building materials i in Germany for projects worldwide since 1936. KORODUR recognised early on that 3D concrete printing is a fascinating technology for all of us. Please feel free to contact us here if you have specific questions about our products.

As a company, we recognized early on that 3D concrete printing is an exciting technology. Together with CyBe Construction, we have developed our own 3D technology.

CyBe Construction is a technology company for the construction industry. Since 2013, Berry Hendriks, CEO and founder of CyBe Construction, has developed hardware, software, building materials and a digital learning platform, making it a one-stop store for construction innovations such as 3D concrete printing and parametric design, and a leader in the industry.
