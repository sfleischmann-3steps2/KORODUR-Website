# Sportano, Retail outlet

## Prosa (EN)

Sportano.pl, an innovative online multibrand sports shop, has opened a high-tech stationary shop in Warsaw's largest retail park Homepark Targówek.
Congratulations!

You can see our TRU PC GRAY – it is a high quality, innovative, self-leveling, mineral, decorative screed, based on special cement technology, in polished concrete optics. TRU PC is designed to simulate the appearance of polished concrete. This product is perfect for polished, decorative screeds. For attractive, unique design of sales rooms, restaurants, foyers, museums, administrative buildings, schools, airports as well as any prestigious interiors.

Qualified for indoor and outdoor use, also in wet areas.
